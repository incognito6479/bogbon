Cooperation

Currently, our laboratory cooperates with the Samarkand Institute of Veterinary Medicine, the Tashkent State Agrarian University and the Center for Genomics and Bioinformatics of the Academy of Sciences of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Territory

The agricultural complex covers a total area of 24.04 hectares, including a laboratory for 0.24 hectares of land and a modern greenhouse for 1 hectare. Seedlings of 32 varieties of fruit trees that are not found in the Republic of Uzbekistan were brought and grown on 22 hectares of land.

Staff

Five biotechnologists and agrochemists of the laboratory have returned from training at the BETA FIDANCHILIK in vitro laboratory in Adana, Turkey, and at the microbiology laboratory in Erdamli.
